CMU Explorer

In conjuction with Yinzcam Inc. and a class at CMU, I worked on a mobile app to create an immersive campus experience. As a team we designed, created, and implemented an application that allowed users to visit and explore Carnegie Mellon Campus interacting with unique content such as VR video, drone video, and library archives. We also implemented Gimble bluetooth beacon technology.

  • Client: Yinzcam & Carnegie Mellon University
  • Date: December 2015
  • Skills: Javascript, CSS, HTML, iOS, & Gimble

The Grass Portrait

Using my physical computing background, I designed and created an interactive art installation for individuals walking through the library. Embedded LEDs would respond to movement and a tiny speaker create sounds of nature to create a relaxing environment. The piece was designed to be a subtle and relaxing piece of the library environment.

  • Client: Carnegie Mellon University
  • Date: October 2015
  • Skills: Arduino, LEDs, Mechanical Oscillators, Band Saw, & Laser Cutter
